Curriculum Settings

Autumn Term
Number and Place Value
3 weeks
Understand place value, read and write numbers, compare and order numbers
Formative quizzes, end of unit test
Addition and Subtraction
4 weeks
Add and subtract numbers, solve problems using addition and subtraction
Problem-solving tasks, end of unit test
Geometry: Properties of Shapes
3 weeks
Identify and describe 2D and 3D shapes, compare and classify shapes
Practical assessment, shape project
Spring Term
Multiplication and Division
4 weeks
Recall multiplication facts, multiply and divide numbers, solve problems
Times tables test, problem-solving assessment
Fractions
3 weeks
Recognize and use fractions, compare and order fractions, add and subtract fractions
Fractions quiz, end of unit test
Measurement
3 weeks
Measure and compare lengths, mass, volume, and time
Practical measurement tasks, end of unit assessment
Summer Term
Statistics
3 weeks
Interpret and present data, solve problems using information in charts and graphs
Data collection project, interpretation tasks
Position and Direction
2 weeks
Describe position, direction, and movement, including rotation
Practical navigation tasks, coordinate grid assessment
Problem Solving and Review
4 weeks
Apply mathematical knowledge to solve problems, review key concepts
End of year assessment, problem-solving project
