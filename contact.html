Frequently Asked Questions

How can I schedule a demo of the platform?

You can request a demo by filling out the contact form on this page or by emailing us directly at demos@edpsychconnect.com. Please include your preferred date and time, and we'll get back to you to confirm.

Do you offer training for educators?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training sessions for educators and institutions. These can be conducted virtually or in-person, depending on your needs and location. Contact our team to learn more about our training options.

Is EdPsych Connect available internationally?

Yes, EdPsych Connect is available worldwide. While our platform is optimized for UK educational standards, it can be adapted for use in other educational systems. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

How do I report a technical issue?

Technical issues can be reported through our support portal at support.edpsychconnect.com or by emailing support@edpsychconnect.com. Please include as much detail as possible, including screenshots if applicable.
