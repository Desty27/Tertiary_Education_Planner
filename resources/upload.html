Resource Upload Guidelines

Quality Standards

All resources should be well-structured, error-free, and provide clear educational value. Resources should include clear objectives, instructions, and where applicable, answer keys or teacher notes.

Copyright and Licensing

Only upload resources that you have created or have explicit permission to share. Properly attribute any third-party content used within your resources. Choose an appropriate license to specify how others can use your resource.

Accessibility

Make your resources accessible to all learners. Use clear fonts, provide alt text for images, and ensure good color contrast. Consider providing alternative formats for different learning needs.

Metadata and Discoverability

Provide accurate and detailed information about your resource, including a descriptive title, comprehensive description, appropriate subject and age group classifications, and relevant tags to help others find your resource.
